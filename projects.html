Soso-Unplugged Tutoring System

The Soso-Unplugged Tutoring System is an advanced online platform designed to streamline the tutoring process for both students and educators. With a focus on user experience, the system facilitates effective learning through various integrated tools and features.

Our platform ensures seamless interaction between tutors and students, enabling efficient schedule management, progress tracking, and communication.

Features

  • Schedule Management: Tools for scheduling sessions, setting reminders, and managing availability to optimize time management.
  • Progress Tracking: Features for monitoring student progress, tracking performance, and providing feedback.
  • Communication Tools: Integrated messaging and video conferencing to facilitate real-time interactions and support.

Technologies Used

  • HTML/CSS
  • JavaScript
  • firebase Auth and Supabase SQL DB

Our platform aims to enhance the tutoring experience by offering a comprehensive set of tools that cater to the needs of both students and tutors, making learning more engaging and effective.
