About Me

Hello! I'm Soluele Mabulu, a passionate software developer based in Potchefstroom, South Africa. Currently, I'm in my final year of pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at North-West University, Potchefstroom. My journey in the tech world has equipped me with strong skills in HTML, CSS, Java, and web design, and I've gained valuable experience working as a student assistant and intern at APJ Automation.

In addition to my technical expertise, I run Tell A Neva Holdings, a Black-owned and managed company that provides services ranging from supply and delivery to web development and graphic design. When I'm not coding or managing my business, you'll find me immersed in my hobbies—whether it's producing music, reading a good book, gaming, or exploring the latest in technology.

Feel free to connect with me if you share similar interests or if you’re interested in collaborating on a project!
